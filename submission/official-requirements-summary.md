# Official Requirements Summary

Checked pages:

- https://hackonvibe.com/
- https://dorahacks.io/hackathon/hackonvibe/detail

## Event

HackOnVibe is an online hackathon for AI-assisted builders. The event page describes the goal as building an AI-assisted everyday tool for individuals, small teams, or small and medium-sized businesses.

## Timing

The event runs from June 5 to June 7, 2026. The official page uses EEST for schedule items.

Key schedule items shown on the official page:

- Kickoff call: June 5, 2026 at 19:00 EEST
- Mentor sessions: June 6, 2026 from 18:00 to 19:00 EEST
- Pitch masterclass: June 7, 2026 at 12:00 EEST
- Final submission deadline: June 7, 2026 at 18:00 EEST
- Project pitching: June 7, 2026 at 18:30 EEST
- Results: June 7, 2026 at 20:30 EEST

## Submission Requirements

The submission should include:

- product description
- working demo or prototype
- target users
- core functionality
- AI-powered features
- user flow or key product screens
- business model and monetization idea
- go-to-market strategy or first-user acquisition plan
- explanation of what was built during the hackathon
- future development roadmap

## Judging Criteria

The official DoraHacks page lists these evaluation areas:

- Usefulness
- Functionality
- AI implementation
- Business potential
- Execution
- Presentation
- Community validation

ServerScout AI Risk Briefing Assistant fits the event because it is a small AI-assisted workflow for a recurring small-team problem: turning scan evidence into understandable remediation decisions and report-ready language.
